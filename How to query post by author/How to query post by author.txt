<p>First, click on the <strong>Query Post</strong> menu.<br>Now search for the author by using the<strong> Choose</strong> button. Here you will find four options; click your desired option.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<p><strong>For Author:</strong><br>You can access the author dropdown menu by choosing the author. Next, type in the <strong>Author ID</strong>. According to the author's id, your Post Grid will update.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<p><strong>For Author Name:</strong><br>You can access the "<strong>Author Name</strong>" dropdown menu by choosing the author name. Next, type in the author's name. According to the author's name, your Post Grid will update.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<p><strong>For Author In:</strong><br>Here type the <strong>Author ID</strong>s <em>separated by a comma</em>. The post grid is then updated with the specified authors' posts.</p>
